04 / ABOUT

hotel-hacker is a small, honest tool that finds the actually-cheapest hotel for any trip you give it — not just the lowest sticker price, but the cheapest after everything that quietly changes the math: loyalty-points redemptions, free-night perks (Marriott fifth-night-free, IHG fourth-night-free, Hilton fifth-free), Amex Fine Hotels & Resorts credits, refundability, and currency haircuts.

It runs locally on your computer. There is no account, no tracking, no upsell. It uses Google Hotels (via SerpApi's free tier) for the listings, applies a transparent ranking formula, and explains every dollar of the result in one plain-English sentence.

HOW IT WORKS

  1. You enter where and when

    Destination, dates, who's coming.

  2. We pull real rates

    From Google Hotels (one search of your 250/month free quota).

  3. We re-rank by effective cost

    Raw price minus loyalty-point value, minus free-night value, minus Amex Fine Hotels & Resorts credit value, plus currency haircuts on non-USD folios, plus a small penalty for non-refundable rates.

  4. You see one explanation per row

    Each row shows raw price, effective price, and a one-sentence reason citing the exact line items applied. Click any row to expand the full math grid.

WHAT THIS DOES NOT DO

Flights. For award-flight searching, see flight_hacker — the sibling project this one was modeled after.
