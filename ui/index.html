04 / ABOUT

hotel-hacker is a small, honest tool that finds the actually-cheapest hotel for any trip you give it — not just the lowest sticker price, but the cheapest after everything that quietly changes the math: points redemptions, free-night perks, FHR credits, refundability, and currency haircuts.

It runs locally on your computer. There is no account, no tracking, no upsell. It uses Google Hotels (via SerpApi's free tier) for the listings, applies a transparent ranking formula, and explains every dollar of the result in one plain-English sentence.

HOW IT WORKS

  1. You enter where and when

    Destination, dates, who's coming.

  2. We pull real rates

    From Google Hotels (one search of your 250/month free quota).

  3. We re-rank by effective cost

    Raw price minus points value, minus free-night value, minus FHR credits, plus currency haircuts and flexibility penalty.

  4. You see one explanation per row

    "$840 raw − $100 FHR credit + $6 currency haircut = $746 net via Amex FHR." Click any row for the full math.

WHAT THIS DOES NOT DO

Flights. For award-flight searching, see flight_hacker — the sibling project this one was modeled after.
